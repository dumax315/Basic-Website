Join the Beginner Coding Club

The Beginner Coding Club will help you grasp the basics of coding while creating projects you can show off. This club will help you get prepared for harder coding classes like AP Computer Science. It hopes to have the benefits of peer support and semi-structure without the stress of grades, due dates, and assignments.

Meetings will be from 9:45 to 10:40 on Wednesdays and the club will start in 2nd semester.
